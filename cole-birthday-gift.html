During validation, should the model behave the same as during training? Think: dropout, batch norm...
✅ Correct!! model.eval() disables dropout and fixes batch norm stats. Without it, validation runs under training-mode randomness. Also torch.no_grad() is missing but eval() is the main bug. Genuinely impressive 🌸
